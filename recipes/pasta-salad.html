Pasta Salad

Description

This pasta salad is an easy dish for those who want to an easy to make but still delicious meal!

It's a colorful dish full with chopped vegetables, delicious sauce, and wonderful pasta that'll crave your tastes, and will stay good in the fridge for a reasonable amount of time!

Ingredients

For this pasta salad, you'll need:

  • 1 pound of tri-colored spiral pasta
  • 1 16-ounce bottle of Zesty Italian dressing
  • 2 1/2 cups of diced heirloom tomatoes
  • 1 diced red bell pepper
  • 2 1/2 cups of diced ham
  • 2 cups of diced cucumber
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. When brought to a boil, pour the pasta into the pot and follow the cooking instructions found on the pasta box.
  2. While the pasta cooks, mix the rest of your ingredients (except for the dressing and seasoning for now) into a bowl.
  3. Drain the pasta once cooked, and mix it with your bowl of ingredients.
  4. Once mixed, add in the seasoning and dressing.
  5. If you want to dig in now, go for it! Optionally, if you like your salad chilled, cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate it for at least 4 hours.
  6. Enjoy!
