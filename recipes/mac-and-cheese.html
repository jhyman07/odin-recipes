Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Macaroni and cheese is a dish for the whole family! It's a delicious and gooey pasta that'll be sure to crave your hunger!

It's a good choice for cooking in bulk that'll last a few days in the fridge, and anyone can make it!

Ingredients

For this macaroni and cheese dish, you'll need:

  • 1 16 ounce box of elbow macaroni
  • 1 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 16 ounce package of shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 whisked eggs
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 5 ounce can of evaporated milk
  • 1 12 ounce can of condensed Cheddar cheese soup
  • Salt, pepper, and paprika to taste

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with heavily salted water and bring to a boil, then cook the pasta to the package's directions.
  2. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and transfer the pasta to a slow cooker.
  3. Add the butter and stir the pasta until the butter is fully melted, and add salt and pepper. Sprinkle half of the Cheddar cheese to the pasta and stir.
  4. In another bowl, whisk the evaporated milk and eggs together until the mixture is smooth. Then, add the pasta into the mixture.
  5. In another bowl, whisk the milk and condensed soup together until the mixture is smooth, then add this to the pasta mixture.
  6. Sprinkle the rest of the cheese into the mixture.
  7. Allow the slow cooker to cook the pasta on the low seting for 3 hours.
  8. After the 3 hours is up, turn the cooker off and serve! Transfer to a bowl with plastic wrap into the fridge to save any leftovers.
