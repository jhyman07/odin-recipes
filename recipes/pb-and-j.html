Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

This is a classic sandwich that only requires a minimal amount of ingredients, and is very quick to prepare!

It's very customizable according to your own tastes, and anyone can put their own twist on it!

Ingredients

  • A loaf of bread
  • A jar of peanut butter
  • A jar of any flavor of jelly

Steps

  1. Take out two pieces of bread and lay them flat on a plate. Optionally, for added texture, you can toast each piece of bread!
  2. Use a butter knife and take out a glob of peanut butter and spread it on a piece of bread.
  3. Do this again on the other piece of bread with your jelly.
  4. Once satisfied, combine the breads together and enjoy your sandwich!
